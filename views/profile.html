who we are


"Whether you are looking to adopt or hoping to find a new home for your furry friend, paws is your definitive resource."


paws is the result of three devastatingly attractive and unequivocally brilliant web developers. CEO Chris Chan originated the vision to build the best pet adoption service on the web. As fellow animal enthusiasts, COO Toby Zitsman and CTO Natalia Hess were eager to join Chris on this endeavor. Many cups of coffee later, we finally launched our site. We love our pets and can't wait for you to find yours.
